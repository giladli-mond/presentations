Recommended Next Steps
Activation
Focus onboarding investment on the first 7 days
54% of churns never sent a campaign, and 81% left within 4 months. The new onboarding flow (Post-OB) is showing early promise on activation rates. Doubling down on getting users to send their first campaign within the first week is the highest-leverage move.
Outreach
Reach out to Account 317043 and 28873192
These two active senders who churned are strong candidates for the churn feedback email workflow. Account 317043 was clearly getting value. Account 28873192 had the highest ARR among active senders.
Early Warning
Run the risk model monthly and set up proactive outreach
The 81% prediction accuracy validates the model. Consider a monthly sweep of CRITICAL/HIGH accounts with automated or semi-automated outreach (e.g. an in-app prompt, email nudge, or CSM alert for Enterprise accounts).
Enterprise
Prioritise Enterprise yearly accounts in risk monitoring
6 Enterprise yearly accounts churned $14.3k. At $2,378 avg ARR, each one is worth significant retention effort. Even saving 2-3 of these per month would meaningfully reduce ARR churn.
