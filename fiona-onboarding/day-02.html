A team value

We give a duck.

🦆

Give a duck about everything.

It sounds simple. It is, actually. We care about the quality of what we ship. We care whether users actually get value from it. We care when something is off and someone is not okay. We care enough to say something when we think there is a better way.

It does not mean being precious or anxious about every tiny thing. It means showing up with genuine investment in what you are doing. The duck on your desk is the team's way of saying: your contribution matters here, so bring all of it.
